Paradigm - Quel'Thalas

Paradigm of Quel'thalas is a 2 night raiding guild formed by a group of friends with over a decade of Cutting Edge experience on this realm.

During progression our raiding environment is focused, calm and cooperative however, we also enjoy having a laugh during less serious content.
