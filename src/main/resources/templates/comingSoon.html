Paradigm - Quel'Thalas


Paradigm of Quel'thalas is a 2 night raiding guild formed by a group of friends with over a decade of Cutting Edge experience on this realm.
During progression our raiding environment is focused, calm and cooperative however, we also enjoy having a laugh during less serious content.


Our Raid Times Are:
Wednesday 19:45-23:00 ST (CET)
Sunday 19:15-22:30 ST (CET)


We are currently looking for 1 or 2 experienced, skilled and likeminded individuals to complete our raid team:
Holy Paladin - Warlock
Whilst our preference is as listed above,
do not be put off if you feel Paradigm is the guild for you!
Exceptional applicants will always be considered.
